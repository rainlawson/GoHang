Welcome to GoHang, my experimental implementation of Hangman in Go.

Controls:
- Esc: Go back to the main menu -
- Enter a letter to guess -

Rules:
- Press "Game" on main menu to start a new game -
- Guess the word by entering letters one at a time -
- You have a limited number of incorrect guesses before you lose -
- Correct guesses reveal the letter in the word -
- Incorrect guesses draw a part of the hangman -
- The game ends when you guess the word or run out of guesses -

FAIR WARNING:
- If you back out of a game, you will lose your progress, and a new word will be chosen -
